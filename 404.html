Page Reported Missing; Search Called Off at Dawn

By The Night Editor · Filed Hastily · Camarines Norte
Readers who followed the link have arrived to find only the outline of a page, a faint smell of ink, and a hand-written note that reads simply: “GONE TO PRESS WITHOUT RETURN.”

404

— THE URL WAS LAST SEEN WALKING TOWARD THE PROVINCES —

A spokesman for the newsroom, reached late last evening at an establishment this paper shall not name, confirmed that the dispatch had been filed, reviewed, and then “seemingly absorbed by the typesetter himself.” Attempts to retrieve it have been unproductive.

We offer the following reassurances to the bereaved reader:

  • The link may have moved (under new management)
    Many of our back issues have been reorganised since the last press run.
  • The link may have retired (honourably)
    Some columns run their course and are given a quiet funeral.
  • The link was, in fact, a typo (no offence)
    The Editor is a fallible creature. So is the URL bar.
