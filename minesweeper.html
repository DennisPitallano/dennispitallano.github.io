How to Play
  • Left-click (or tap) a cell to reveal it.
  • Right-click (or toggle Flag Mode then tap) to plant or remove a flag.
  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent. Use logic to find them all.
  • Chord: click a revealed number whose flag count matches to clear its safe neighbors.
  • The first click is always safe.
